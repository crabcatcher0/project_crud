About Us

Triyuga Janta Multiple Campus, established in 1987 (2044 BS), stands as a distinguished public educational institution situated in Gaighat, Udayapur district, Nepal. Committed to academic excellence, the campus is affiliated with Tribhuvan University and holds recognition from the University Grants Commission (UGC) of Nepal. Our campus serves as a cornerstone for higher education, offering a diverse range of bachelor's degree programs to cater to the educational aspirations of our students. These programs include Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.), One Year Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.), Bachelor of Education in Information Communication and Technology (B.Ed. ICT), Bachelor of Arts (BA), Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS), and master's degree programs such as Master of Education (M.Ed.). Triyuga Janta Multiple Campus is proud to be affiliated with the National Examinations Board (NEB), providing ten plus two programs in Education, Management, and Humanities. Our commitment to quality education, coupled with a focus on holistic development, reflects our dedication to nurturing the next generation of leaders and scholars.
